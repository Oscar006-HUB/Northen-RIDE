Terms & Conditions

1. Booking Policy

  • Bookings are non-refundable but can be rescheduled with 24 hours' notice.
  • Passengers must present valid ID upon boarding.
  • Seats are assigned at booking and cannot be shared.

2. Payment Policy

  • Full payment is required at the time of booking.
  • Mobile money payments must be confirmed within 10 minutes.
  • Failed payments may result in cancellation.

3. Travel Policy

  • Arrive at least 30 minutes before departure.
  • Luggage is limited to one medium-sized bag per passenger.
  • North Link reserves the right to modify schedules due to unforeseen circumstances.

4. Privacy Policy

  • We do not share personal information with third parties.
  • Contact details may be used for travel updates and promotions.
